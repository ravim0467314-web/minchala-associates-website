LLP Compliance Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A limited liability partnership checklist for partner records, annual filing, books, tax and event-based compliance.

Checklist
1. Maintain LLP agreement, partner details, contribution records and registered office proof.
2. Keep books, bank statements, invoices and expense records updated.
3. Review Form 11, Form 8 and income-tax return due dates as applicable.
4. Track partner admission, resignation, contribution and agreement changes.
5. Check GST, TDS, professional tax and audit applicability based on facts.
6. Retain filed forms, challans and acknowledgements for records.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
