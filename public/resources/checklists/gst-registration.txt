GST Registration Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A document-readiness checklist for GST registration applications and business-profile review.

Checklist
1. Confirm GST applicability based on turnover, supply type and place of supply.
2. Keep PAN, Aadhaar, constitution documents and authorised signatory details ready.
3. Prepare address proof, rent/ownership documents and utility bills for principal place of business.
4. Compile bank proof, photographs and business activity details.
5. Review additional place of business, goods/services and state-specific facts.
6. Retain ARN, registration certificate and login credentials securely.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
