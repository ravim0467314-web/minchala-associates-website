MSME / Business Compliance Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A practical checklist for MSME registration, accounting discipline, GST/TDS, payroll and recurring business compliance.

Checklist
1. Keep Udyam/MSME registration details and business constitution documents updated.
2. Maintain sales, purchases, expenses, bank records and monthly reconciliations.
3. Review GST, TDS, payroll, professional tax and local registration applicability.
4. Track receivables, payables, inventory and statutory dues through a monthly close process.
5. Retain contracts, invoices, purchase orders and compliance acknowledgements.
6. Review compliance calendar and periodic management reports for decision support.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
