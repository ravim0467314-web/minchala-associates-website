Startup / Company Incorporation Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A founder-readiness checklist for entity structure, name reservation, incorporation documents and post-incorporation compliance.

Checklist
1. Discuss entity type, shareholding, directors, registered office and business objects.
2. Keep PAN, identity, address proof and digital signature inputs ready.
3. Prepare name options, MOA/AOA inputs and subscriber/director information.
4. Compile registered office proof, NOC and utility bill records.
5. Plan PAN, TAN, bank account, GST, MSME and professional tax applicability after incorporation.
6. Maintain incorporation certificate, statutory registers and first board documentation.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
