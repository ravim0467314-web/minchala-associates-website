Income Tax Return Filing Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A taxpayer-friendly checklist for salaried, business, professional, capital-gains and other ITR preparation records.

Checklist
1. Collect PAN, Aadhaar, bank details and current contact information.
2. Review Form 16, Form 26AS, AIS/TIS and tax payment challans.
3. Compile salary, business, profession, house property, capital gains and other income records.
4. Keep deduction, investment, loan interest, insurance and donation proofs ready.
5. Reconcile TDS/TCS credits, advance tax and self-assessment tax payments.
6. Retain computation, acknowledgement and filed return copy for records.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
