GST Return Filing Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A monthly GST return preparation checklist for outward supplies, ITC review, GSTR-2B matching and portal records.

Checklist
1. Reconcile outward sales with invoices, credit notes, debit notes and books.
2. Match input tax credit with GSTR-2B, vendor invoices and books.
3. Review reverse charge, exempt supplies, nil-rated supplies and advances where applicable.
4. Check e-invoice, e-way bill and HSN/SAC reporting requirements.
5. Review GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B values before submission.
6. Save filed returns, challans, ledgers and reconciliation notes.

Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope.
