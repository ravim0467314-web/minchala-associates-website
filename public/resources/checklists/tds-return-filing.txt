TDS Return Filing Checklist
Minchala & Associates, Chartered Accountants

A TDS compliance checklist for deductor details, challans, PAN validation, returns and correction tracking.

Checklist
1. Confirm TAN, deductor details, responsible person and period of return.
2. Reconcile expense ledgers, salary records and TDS deduction entries.
3. Verify PAN, section, rate, threshold and lower/nil deduction certificates where applicable.
4. Match challans, BSR codes, dates and amounts before return preparation.
5. Review Form 24Q/26Q/27Q/27EQ applicability and validation reports.
6. Track acknowledgements, defaults, correction statements and Form 16/16A issuance.

Disclaimer: This checklist is general educational guidance only and is not professional advice. Requirements depend on facts, current law, portal records, due dates, notifications and engagement scope. A formal professional engagement begins only after acceptance, independence/conflict checks and document review.
